Press Release Oct 24, 2025 Financial Express

Arop Group sets new benchmark in elevator technology innovation.

DHAKA – Arop Group, a prominent provider of vertical transportation solutions, today announced a significant breakthrough in elevator technology that promises to transform urban mobility across Bangladesh.

The new system, unveiled during a tech showcase in the capital, represents a fusion of high-speed mechanical engineering and intelligent software control. By focusing on "Zero-Friction" energy efficiency, Arop Group has developed a platform that reduces environmental impact while dramatically improving passenger wait times during peak building traffic.

"Our latest innovation is more than just a faster lift; it's a smarter lift. This technology allows buildings to handle high volumes of traffic more efficiently than ever before, using data to predict where passengers need to go before they even push a button." — S. Islam, Chief Technology Officer

In addition to performance gains, the systems are equipped with a new generation of regenerative drives. These units capture energy that would normally be wasted during braking and feed it back into the building’s power system, potentially reducing elevator-related energy costs by up to 25%.

Key Technological Highlights

  • Predictive Dispatching: AI algorithms minimize wait times by positioning cabs based on historical usage patterns.
  • Regenerative Braking: Converts kinetic energy back into usable electricity.
  • Ultra-Quiet Operation: Advanced insulation and vibration-dampening materials for a seamless ride.

Arop Group continues to lead the market by bringing international standards to the local engineering sector. This new benchmark technology is expected to be rolled out in premium corporate developments starting early next year.
