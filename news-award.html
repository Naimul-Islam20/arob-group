Award Recognition Sep 12, 2025 The Daily Star

Excellence in Engineering Award 2025 goes to Arop Group.

DHAKA – In a prestigious ceremony held last night, Arop Group was honored with the "Excellence in Engineering Award 2025," recognizing its outstanding contribution to vertical transportation solutions and safety standards in Bangladesh.

The award, presented by the National Engineering Association, highlights Arop Group's commitment to implementing international safety protocols and its success in executing complex engineering projects with precision and reliability. This recognition reinforces Arop Group's position as a market leader in the construction and transportation sector.

"Receiving this award is a testament to the hard work and dedication of our entire engineering team. At Arop Group, safety and quality are not just goals; they are the foundation of everything we build." — S. Islam, Director of Engineering

Commitment to Safety Standards

Arop Group has consistently pioneered the adoption of multi-beam infrared sensors and energy-efficient synchronous motors, ensuring that every lift system exceeds local safety requirements and matches global standards. The company's rigorous testing and maintenance protocols were specifically called out by the judging panel as a benchmark for the industry.

As the skyline of Bangladesh continues to rise, Arop Group remains dedicated to providing the reliable lifelines that modern buildings require. This award serves as both a milestone and a motivation to continue pushing the boundaries of engineering excellence.
