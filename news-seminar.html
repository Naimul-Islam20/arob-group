International Event Aug 05, 2025 Dhaka Tribune

Arop Group holds international seminar on modern urban mobility.

DHAKA – Experts from around the world gathered in the capital today for an international seminar hosted by Arop Group to discuss the future of urban mobility and the role of intelligent lift systems in mega-cities.

The seminar featured keynote speakers from leading architectural firms and urban planning departments, focusing on how smart elevators can contribute to sustainable city development. The event served as a platform for sharing global best practices and exploring the integration of IoT technology in high-rise buildings.

"As cities grow vertically, the way we manage traffic within buildings becomes as critical as road infrastructure. Our goal is to create a seamless transit experience that connects the street to the top floor." — International Guest Speaker

Vertical Transportation & Mega-Cities

Discussions centered on the integration of AI and predictive maintenance in vertical transportation, as well as the aesthetic impact of elevator design on modern architecture. Arop Group showcased its latest research on touch-less interfaces and high-speed energy recovery systems.

The seminar concluded with a consensus that the next generation of urban mobility will be defined by connectivity and efficiency. Arop Group committed to leading this charge by investing in research and development that addresses the unique challenges of the local urban landscape.
